Privacy Policy – MathOps

Effective Date: 15 January 2026

MathOps (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the MathOps mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored securely in encrypted/hashed form)

App Usage Information

  • App interactions (question attempts, test submissions, progress)
  • Other actions (login activity, account updates, purchases)

Financial Information

  • Purchase history (for managing premium access)

We do not collect or store credit/debit card details. All payments are processed securely by Google Play.

Device Information

  • Device or other identifiers used for app functionality, security, and fraud prevention

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or audio files
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data
  • Advertising or marketing data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and management
  • Providing core app functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Managing purchases and premium features

4. Data Security

All data is transmitted securely using encryption. Passwords are stored using secure hashing techniques.
